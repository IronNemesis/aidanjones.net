The Sun Also Rises

Rating: ★☆☆☆☆

Review

Read this from a beautiful volume of 4 Hemingway novels I found at a garage sale for $3 (still in the shrink wrap!)

They drink alcohol then they go fishing then they watch bull fighting and they drink alcohol. Hemingway has done it again!

On the edge of my seat the whole time waiting for them to finish fishing so they can drink more alcohol and leave the waiter a big tip!
